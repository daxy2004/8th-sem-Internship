# 🌿 House of Abundance Foundation — NGO Website

A fully responsive, multi-page website for the **House of Abundance Foundation**, a non-profit organisation dedicated to empowering women, children, and communities through education, skill development, and equal opportunity.

Built with **React 19 / TypeScript / Vite** during the 8th Semester Industry Internship.

---

## 🏛️ About the Organisation

The **House of Abundance Foundation** is built on four pillars:

> **Education · Equality · Environment · Empowerment**

The foundation works with children, youth, and women from underserved communities to ensure that lack of money, exposure, or opportunity never limits their potential. Key initiatives include:

- **Scholarship Programme** — Supporting deserving students to complete their education with dignity
- **Skill Development Programmes** — Equipping young people and women with digital, technical, and workplace-ready skills
- **Women Empowerment Programme** — Helping women restart livelihoods and achieve financial independence
- **School Transformation Programme** — Creating safe, inclusive, and future-ready learning spaces

## 🛠️ What We Built

A fully responsive **Single Page Application (SPA)** that serves as the digital face of the House of Abundance Foundation. The website:

- Presents the foundation's **mission, vision, and impact** on the homepage
- Provides an in-depth **Who We Are** page detailing the organisation's story and values
- Showcases all **Our Programmes** — Scholarship, Skill Development, School Transformation, and Women Empowerment
- Includes a **Work With Us** page for volunteers and open positions
- Features a **Donate** page with giving options and impact transparency
- Has a **Contact Us** page for outreach and partnerships
- Displays an **auto-scrolling partners carousel** showcasing collaborating organisations
- Features **testimonials** from real beneficiaries and partner educators
